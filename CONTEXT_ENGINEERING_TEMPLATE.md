## Usage Tips

1. **Tailor each section** to your specific needs—not every section applies to every task
2. **Be specific over general**—concrete examples beat abstract descriptions
3. **Iterate and refine**—context improves with feedback and updates
4. **Keep it organized**—clear structure makes context easy to follow
5. **Document assumptions**—make implicit knowledge explicit
6. **Include examples**—show, don't just tell
7. **Update regularly**—keep context in sync with project reality
